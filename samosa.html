samosa Recipe

home

make your own home made samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for dough)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Steps

  1. Take flour in a bowl, add salt and oil. Mix well.
  2. Add water slowly and knead into a firm dough. Let it rest for 20 minutes.
  3. Mash boiled potatoes and mix with peas.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, then add potato mixture.
  5. Add spices and cook for 5 minutes. Let filling cool.
  6. Divide dough into small balls and roll into oval shapes.
  7. Cut in half, make a cone, and fill with potato mixture.
  8. Seal edges with water.
  9. Deep fry in medium heat until golden brown.

Serving

Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
